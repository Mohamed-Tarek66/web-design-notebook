The details tag is used to provide a head with a side arrow before it. The arrow is used to show & hide the containt of the details leaving only the head. The summary tag is used to provide the head of the details. The structure of the details code is shown in the following example.

<details>
  <summary>The head of the details</summary>
  The content of the details
</details>
The head of the details The content of the details

A common attribute used in conjuction with the details tag is open and is used to make the details open at the load or reload of the website. This attribute is shown in the following example.

<details open>
  <summary>The head of the details</summary>
  The content of the details
</details>
The head of the details The content of the details
